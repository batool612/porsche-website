Our History

The story of Porsche began in 1948 when Ferdinand Porsche unveiled the first-ever Porsche vehicle, the 356 "No. 1" Roadster. This seminal moment marked the birth of a company that would revolutionize the automotive industry with its dedication to engineering excellence and racing prowess. Over the years, Porsche has continued to push the boundaries of automotive innovation, introducing legendary models such as the 911, 944, 918 Spyder, and the Panamera, each one a testament to our unwavering commitment to perfection.
